About Space Debris Portal 🚀

Advanced orbital debris monitoring and analysis platform

Our Mission

The Space Debris Portal is designed to provide comprehensive monitoring, analysis, and visualization of orbital debris in Earth's orbit. We help space agencies, researchers, and organizations track and understand the growing challenge of space debris through real-time data analytics.

Key Features

  • Real-time orbital debris tracking and monitoring
  • Advanced data visualization and analytics
  • Comprehensive dataset with 1000+ debris objects
  • Country-wise contribution analysis
  • Altitude grouping and trend analysis
  • Interactive dashboard with dynamic charts
  • Upload and analyze custom datasets

Data & Analytics

Our platform processes extensive datasets of orbital objects including payloads, debris fragments, and rocket bodies. We provide detailed insights into debris distribution patterns, growth trends, and spatial characteristics to support research and decision-making.

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies for performance, reliability, and scalability. Our platform utilizes Django for backend services, MongoDB for data persistence, and Chart.js for interactive visualizations.
